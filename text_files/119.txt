Money to Pawn Don't get fooled at the pawn shop when a seller hands Chumlee a thirty dollar bill. Will this curious cash have Chum reaching for his wallet to buy it or will it turn out to be a costly currency that's not worth making a deal? Next, sparks will fly when Rick considers restoring a worse for wear spark plug cleaner made by Albert Champion. Later, Rick hires a small team of experts to draw up a value for a Walt Disney signed copy of The Art of Animation. Will a spoonful of sugar make the deal go down or will a no deal leave Rick singing hakuna matata?
