Spruce Goose Wing it with the Pawn Stars when a test piece of Howard Hughes' Spruce Goose flies into the shop. Will Rick and Chumlee take off with a part of this famous aircraft or will the asking price be sky high? Then, Rick sees stars when celebrity paintings by artist Stephen Fishwick are brought in. Can he see the big picture or will he paint himself into a corner? And later, it's any man's game when the guys make a wager over which donut Rick will eat. Will one of them scoop the pot or will Rick put an end to their hunger games
