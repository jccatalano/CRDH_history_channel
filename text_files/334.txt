Go For Chum Raise a glass with the Pawn Stars when Rick spots an authentic moonshine still. Will he be able to see straight through the negotiation or will end up drowning his sorrows? Then, Corey and Chumlee are star struck when a man brings in a huge collection of 1940s and 50s celebrity autographed postcards. Containing some of the biggest names in Hollywood, will the postcards be "Return To Sender" or will the guys be thanking their lucky stars? And later, Rick gets hung up on Chumlee's not so subtle phone calls during work. Will Chumlee get the message or send him straight to voicemail?
