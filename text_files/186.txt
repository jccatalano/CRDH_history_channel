Anytime, Any Mace Prepare for battle with the Pawn Stars as Rick checks out a medieval mace. Will it be a fair fight for this primitive weapon or is he in for a low blow? And later, the Old Man sends Corey and Chum on a simple errand that turns into a daylong adventure. Will they make it back in one piece?
