London Pawning Pawn Stars goes international when Rick takes a trip to England and visits his buddy's pawn shop. Will Rick feel outclassed when he discovers that Regal Pawn owns a piece of the Titanic? Then, back in Las Vegas someone wants to buy Rick's favorite mini-bike, a Rupp Roadster. Will the guys have the guts to sell it while Rick is out of the country? And later, Rick finds some rare maps of Nevada and North America. But with non-negotiable prices, will he take his business elsewhere?
