Pawns Ahoy! Sail away with the Pawn Stars as a peg leg pistol hobbles its way into the shop. Will Rick and Corey say, "ahoy" or will they toss it overboard if it doesn't fire? Then Corey and Chum check out four yearbooks signed by the ultimate teenager, James Dean. Will Corey be a rebel without a cause and turn down the offer or will he be a cool kid and make a sale?
